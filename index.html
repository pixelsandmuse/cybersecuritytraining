The Stakes

Simple mistakes can lead to catastrophic results:

  • Financial and data loss
  • Identity theft
  • Organization-wide compromises

Cyber threats are ubiquitous, targeting individuals every single day.

What is Phishing?

A cyberattack that tricks users into revealing sensitive information through deceptive communications.

Common Channels:
Emails, SMS (Smishing), and Fake Websites.

Scenario: Email Investigation

Examine this email carefully. Look at the sender, the tone, and the links.

From: support@paypal-security.net
Subject: Action Required: Account Suspension
Dear Customer,

Your account has been temporarily suspended due to suspicious activity. Please verify your identity immediately to prevent permanent closure.

Verify Your Account Now

Password Hygiene

Strong passwords are your first line of personal defense.

Length
Use at least 12-16 characters.
Complexity
Mix letters, numbers, and symbols.

Crucial Rule: Never reuse the same password across multiple accounts.

Beyond Passwords: MFA

Multi-Factor Authentication adds a critical extra layer. Even if an attacker steals your password, they can't access your account without the second factor.

  • Authenticator Apps (Best)
  • Security Keys
  • SMS Codes (Better than nothing)

Decision Point

You receive a text message from "your bank" asking you to verify a transaction via a provided link. What is the safest action?

Click the link to check the transaction quickly.
Ignore the text entirely and do nothing.
Navigate directly to the bank's official website in your browser.
